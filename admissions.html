Admissions

We welcome students who are passionate about gaining technical skills. Our admissions process is simple and transparent.

Steps to Apply

  1. Choose your desired course.
  2. Fill the application form online.
  3. Attach your KCSE certificate and ID copy.
  4. Pay registration fee via M-Pesa.
